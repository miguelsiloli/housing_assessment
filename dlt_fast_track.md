2. Drive Marketplace Impact (The Partner Pleaser)

Utilization is the baseline, but Marketplace (Business Development) is the "fast track." Volunteer 10-15 hours a year to help write the "Technical Solution" or "Architecture" slides for a high-value FS proposal. If you help a Partner win a €1M contract, your promotion case becomes undeniable.


Identify a specific part of your FS project (e.g., the Databricks Delta Lake implementation or a specific SCADA dataflow) and ask to own the delivery end-to-end. This means you manage the timeline, the technical design, and the primary client contact for that piece.


3. Build a "Reusable Asset" or Accelerator

Deloitte thrives on IP. Build a standard PySpark script, a Databricks notebook template for FS compliance (e.g., IFRS17), or a model monitoring dashboard. Demo this to your AI & Data community. Being the "owner" of a tool that saves other teams 40 hours is the highest form of internal branding.


5. Master "Upward Management" with your Coach

Your Coach is your primary advocate in the promotion room. Meet them monthly (not quarterly). Be explicit: "My goal is to promote to Senior in the next cycle. What are the 3 specific gaps I need to close this month?". Ensure they have "due diligence" (written praise) from your project managers to back you up.
